My research interest lies in the real-world problems solving with machine learning, especially for the scientific application (AI4Science). The ultimate goal of my research is to build AI systems that perform proficiently on domain-specific problems, outperform exact or heuristic solvers and human experts both in efficiency and accuracy. I am excited to witness machine learning to boost the development of science.
