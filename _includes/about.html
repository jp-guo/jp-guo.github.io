About

Hi, there! I am Jinpei Guo, a second-year student in the MSML program at Carnegie Mellon University. My research focuses on low-level computer vision, including image and video restoration, compression, and diffusion-based generative models. I am particularly interested in developing efficient generative frameworks that unify restoration and compression across diverse real-world degradations. I worked closely with Dr. Jian Wang. Prior to that, I received my bachelor’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I worked with Prof. Junchi Yan and collaborated with Prof. Xujie Si.
